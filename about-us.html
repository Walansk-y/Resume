Wallys World?

Wallys World has been serving musicians at all levels for more than 30 years. Our journey began over spring break when our Web Designer Walansky Richemond, working out of a dorm room with sub-par internet decided to work on his project.Since then Wallys World has grown into the premiere catalog and Internet retailer serving musicians from flutists to tuba players, and percussion artists all alike.
